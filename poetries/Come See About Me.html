Come See About Me

I was looking at you, looking at me
Your eyes had always been my focus
I knew it wasn't a phase when I felt what I felt
When you held my hand
Smiled and rested your head on my shoulders

The invisible strings
That you certainly knew how to pull
I gazed wide-eyed
As my Cotton scarf with turquoise dye
Unravelled line by line

These days, I dont feel alive anymore
And to every new passenger have I lied
They dont know much about you
And now neither do I

Over the years we grew apart but
Your number has always been on my favourites' list
The 'hello' is still the same
Once a month when the phone rings
Five years of memory
The good, the ugly and the turbulent

My days, some are good, some not so fine
When I wake up from my lonely nightmares
Yours is the only name that comes to my mind

Five years later, Last night You walked up to me
and hugged me in my dreams
Five years felt like yesterday
And now yesterday seems centuries away
Maybe you could make some time to come see about me
