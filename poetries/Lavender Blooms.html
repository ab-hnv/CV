Lavender Blooms

Lavender blooms
In a vase in my room
The room is filled with lilac air

I forgive the old coffee stains
The guests don't give me despair

The sepia filtered painting On the north wall
Takes me back to the land of rock and air
The aroma of the ivy on the south window sill
"Don't go down the memory lane" it declares

The storm of memories
that used to storm into my mind
to shatter the glass fence
And leave me scared.
These days I don't plead for silence
or mercy or sympathy
I realised they scare me cause
I am what they fear

The bracelet from the long lost pain Irks my hand
Time to take it off now
Been heavy for a long time
Carrying weights of past affairs

I'm good in my own lilac room
Deep breaths of the lilac air
